Simple extension to move tabs in Opera
Inspired by the Chrome extension Rearrange Tabs (https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/rearrange-tabs/ccnnhhnmpoffieppjjkhdakcoejcpbga)
	
	Windows:
	Use Shift + Alt + Left to move a tab to the left
	Use Shift + Alt + Right to move a tab to the right
	
	Mac:
	Use Shift + Ctrl + Left to move a tab to the left
	Use Shift + Ctrl + Right to move a tab to the right
